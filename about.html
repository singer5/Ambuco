HSE POLICY STATEMENT

HSE PLAN LEADERSHIP AND COMMITMENT
The policy of HSE and manual in our organization is given a priority as the management and staff act as prime movers in all matters relating to HSE in our operations, management of the firm provides adequate resources of HSE issues. The organisation ensures that efficient and component persons are engaged in the handling of issues affecting its HSE is an important aspect of our core business operation. It is the declared policy of Ambuco Cee Int’l Oil and Gas Nig. Ltd to conduct its business and to execute its project in such a way that the environment is not degraded in any form. We shall also aim at ensuring that all our equipment and personnel at wor5k sites are secured through deliberate efforts aimed at ensuring that we enjoy the good will of host community. We realised that all places we work there are people the owners of the land in which we work. These people have feelings which we must take into consideration while planning work. It is also now the policy of the company to appraise each of our operations with a view considering all possible things that could go wrong and to put in place pro-active actions, policies and measures that will ensure that the identified hazards do not occur, it is therefore in realisation of this prime objective that a HASE manual has been drawn up. It is the management policy to:

  • Ensures management commitments to safety awareness among our employees.
  • Provide a safe working for all employees and thereby prevent accident.
  • Maintain our plants and equipment properly in other to prevent breakdown and eliminate hazards or accidents.
  • Conduct our business in a most safe and efficient manner to prevent injury to the staff and conserve the company’s material resources, by appraising all our operations to identify all possible hazards.
  • Train our employees in order to safely create awareness in them that will enable them recognize unsafe situation and take steps to eliminate the hazards and so prevent accidents.
  • Put in place HSE management structure to ensure the realization of the above objective.
  • Since HSE cost money, to set aside adequate resources that will help in the resources that will help in the realization of the stated objective.
  • Carry out public relation actions with host communities to ensure cordial relationship and at the same time make conscious efforts to ensure that our equipment on site are secured.
  • Take all cashes considerations first and foremost in planning all our activities where a hazard is identified and no acceptable prevent able measures is put in place to forestall the occurrence of such hazard is identified and no acceptable preventable measures is put in place.
  • Make conscious effort to identify with the aspiration of host communities through direct assistance to community development efforts.
    • The overall responsibility for HSE is a line management. Accordingly, all categories of staff will be made to be fully committed in ensuring the realization of the stated objectives It is recognised that the occurrence of accident can be eliminated to a large extent if all forms of UNSAFE ACTS are eliminated, and if all possible hazards are identified at the planning stage of projects and if preventive measures are put in place. In order to safeguard losses to life and property (since accident, if allowed to occur causes loss of life and property or injury). Management will constantly monitor laid down HSE regulations to ensure strict adherence by all employees of this company.

    ROAD SAFETY POLICY

    Road safety and the avoidance of all forms of accidents are major areas of concern to management. Management accepts the fact that accidents to occur, but are caused by actions of persons. This is particularly true of road accidents. For this reason adequate attention is given to the condition of vehicles and plants, journey management and health condition of drivers and operators, so as to ensure that no journey is undertaken unless a proper assessment of the proposed road condition of the vehicle to be used for the journey must be ascertained and also the health condition of the driver is not to be overlooked made.
    All light vehicle drivers and front seat passengers must fasten their seat belts while the vehicle is in motion, speed limits relative road condition must be adhered to, all traffic rules and regulations observed speed limit stands at 100km/hr maximum. Maintenance schedules is drawn up for plants and vehicles are in good working condition at all times, paying special attention to brake, steering alignments, suspension, horn and tyres. Only trained and experienced drivers, who are physically alert are deployed to drive. Drivers must hold valid driving license, and be up to date with traffic codes in force, and be familiar with road signs.
    Heavy duty drivers shall have a motor mate while on duty. All materials to be conveyed must be secured to the vehicle to avoid falling off and causing hazard to life and property. Warning signs and flags or light will be provided where the load being conveyed protrudes. No open body vans, pick-ups, or trippers are to be used for transporting personnel to and from work sites.
